Lyric Guessr

Before You Play:

In order to play this game, you must ensure that your Spotify account is whitelisted on my app.
If this is not the case, please email me at pottermi@dukes.jmu.edu with your Spotify Account email.

Furthermore, this game will not work without the use of a proxy server due to MusixMatch not including CORS information in their headers. Due to the lack of working free proxies, this game relies on CORS anywhere by Rob W in order to get lyrical information. However, this proxy requires an opt-in to function, so please go to https://cors-anywhere.herokuapp.com/ and click their button to opt-in to their demo before playing.
